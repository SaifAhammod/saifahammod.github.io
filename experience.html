  • Android App Developer

    • August 2018 – January 2020
    HOP IN
    Hop IN is a university-based car-sharing startup designed to provide students with a convenient and cost-effective transportation solution. The core concept was to operate private cars along specific routes that end directly at university campuses, allowing students to book and share rides through an Android application. Each car ride was shared by four students traveling in the same direction, offering an efficient alternative to public transport. In the initial phase, we targeted students from NSU, AIUB, and IUB, operating along two main routes: Mirpur 1 to Bashundhara R/A and Uttara to Bashundhara R/A. I was actively involved in managing internal operations and also contributed as a member of the Android development team, working on building and improving the ride-booking app that powered the service.
