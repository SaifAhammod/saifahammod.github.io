About Me

I was born on December 15, 1998, in the vibrant city of Rajshahi, Bangladesh. While my roots are in Rajshahi, I currently live in Dhaka, where I am pursuing my academic and professional goals with dedication and passion.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering, which I completed with the distinction of "Magna Cum Laude." This achievement reflects my commitment to academic excellence. Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science and Engineering at North South University, where I continue to enhance my knowledge and skills in my field of study. In addition to my academic pursuits, I work as a Graduate Teaching Assistant, where I assist in the learning and development of undergraduate students.

My career aspirations are deeply rooted in both researching and teaching. I have a strong research interest in the areas of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, which I believe hold immense potential for shaping the future of technology. Previously, I have had the opportunity to conduct research on Human-Computer Interaction as well as Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, which has strengthened my understanding and passion for these domains. My aim is to contribute to advancements in these fields and share my knowledge through teaching and mentorship.

Beyond academics and work, I have a range of hobbies that keep me engaged and energized. I am passionate about photography, where I capture moments and stories through the lens of my camera. Reading books is another favorite pastime, allowing me to explore new ideas and expand my understanding of various topics. I also enjoy playing video games, which not only offer entertainment but also enhance my strategic thinking and creativity. Watching movies and series is a great way for me to unwind and appreciate different forms of storytelling. Football is a sport I love both watching and playing, as it keeps me active and connected with the thrill of competition.Additionally, I have a deep interest in graphic design, where I enjoy expressing my creativity and experimenting with visual elements. This skill complements my academic background, allowing me to combine technical expertise with artistic expression.

In summary, I am a dedicated individual with a passion for research, teaching, and creativity. My journey continues as I strive to make meaningful contributions to the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, while also enjoying a well-rounded life filled with hobbies and interests that keep me inspired.
